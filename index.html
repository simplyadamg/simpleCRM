What would the advantage be, for a qualified buyer, someone with good credit and plenty of cash , someone who is ready to buy soon... to find your home, out of the thousands available and to come up to your front door and buy your house/condo Well... Let's take your home for example you are selling it for <$450,000 > right? doesn't it make sense to you that a person who has reach a point in their life at which they can buy a home for $450,000 knows that they can walk into any real estate offince in the country and hire a buyer's agent FOR FREE? And look at all of the things that agent will do for them... - They will go through the thousands of home currently available; - They will take them on a door to door tour of the best dozen or so homes - They will negotiate to make the best deal for the buyer - not for you - They will arrange the best financing package for them - They will manage all the details of the entire transaction - And they will probably buy them a closing gift too. All at no cost So... As nice as your house/condo may be... what possible advantage does QUALIFIED buyer have to purchase your home? What I'm asking for is a sit down during which I will share our market research on the comps in your area, and show you how we can make you more money and sell your home faster.
